## 📌 What this app does

Dubago is a web app for AI dubbing. You upload an audio or video file, pick a target language, and the app turns the speech into dubbed audio. It keeps the timing close to the original speech, so the result feels natural.

You sign in with a Google account. Only email addresses on the approved list can use the app. If your account is not on the list, the app blocks access.

## 🧭 What happens during dubbing

The app follows a clear path:

1. It reads your uploaded audio or video
2. It finds the speech in the clip
3. It translates the spoken text
4. It generates new speech in the target language
5. It places the new speech in the same rough time slots as the original
6. It builds the final audio and video files for download

This helps the final dub stay close to the pace of the source clip.

## 🎬 Video and subtitle details

For the final video:
